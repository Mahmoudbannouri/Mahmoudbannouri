## 👋 About Me

Software engineer at **Trends Digital Company** and final-year engineering student at **ESPRIT** (Class of 2026). I take products from **idea to production, end to end** — frontend, mobile, backend, infrastructure and AI — and I keep them running once they're live.

- 🚢 I ship **complete platforms**: web (Next.js/React), mobile (Flutter), backends (Node.js · Express · Prisma) on PostgreSQL & Firestore
- ☁️ I **run what I build**: CI/CD pipelines, Docker, Nginx, PM2, Linux server administration, Google Cloud & Vercel deployments
- 🤖 I build **AI solutions**: LLM-powered features, RAG pipelines, multi-agent workflows, AI content moderation, recommendation engines and n8n automations
- 📐 I design for **scale**: REST APIs, SOA & microservices, caching strategies, real-time systems, SQL + NoSQL — load-tested, not guessed

> Recently shipped: a travel super-app (web + mobile), B2B travel & booking platforms, and AI-powered SaaS products — from architecture to production servers.
